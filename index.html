Approach

Business first. Customer always. AI where it creates leverage.

I do not start with a tool. I start with the customer journey, the decisions teams need to make, and the friction preventing them from doing their best work.

The goal is not to make people work faster. It is to redesign work so people spend more time where human judgment creates value.
Find the frictionMap where customers stall, teams duplicate work, or leadership lacks visibility.
Design the operating modelClarify ownership, milestones, governance, data, and escalation.
Apply AI intentionallyAutomate classification, synthesis, routing, and pattern detection—not relationships.
Measure adoption and impactA system is not successful because it launched. It succeeds when people use it and outcomes improve.
